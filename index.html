Bio

My background is in Quantitative Marketing (Ph.D.), Economics (M.Sc.), and Electrical Engineering (B.Sc.). I love building models that explain real world economic phenomena and using data to estimate and test those models.

My primary research is about the impacts of new technologies on digital platforms. In my job market paper, I quantify the effects of introducing an algorithmic pricing tool on Airbnb and propose solutions to improve the outcomes of the algorithm's deployment.

I will join the Tepper School of Business at Carnegie Mellon University as an Assistant Professor in fall 2023.
