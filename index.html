Background

I got my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and my M.Sc. in Economics from the University of Tehran, Iran. I joined the Marketing PhD program at University of Toronto in 2017.

My research interests are Empirical Industrial Organization, Applied Microeconomics, and Behavioral Economics.
